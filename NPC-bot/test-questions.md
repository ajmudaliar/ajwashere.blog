# Bot Test Questions

Questions used during simulation testing of the NPC bot.

## Identity & Intro
- Hey
- Who are you?
- What do you do?
- Where are you from?
- What's your background?

## Professional & Career
- What does a TPM do?
- How did you get into product?
- What's Botpress?
- What are you working on these days?
- What's your advice for someone early in their career?
- I'm thinking about getting into product management
- I love building stuff but I want to have more impact

## Books & Reading
- What are you reading right now?
- Any book recommendations?
- Shipping products. Any book recommendations for someone getting into PM?

## Travel & Places
- Where have you traveled?
- What's your favorite city you've visited?
- Do you like Montreal?

## Opinions & Hot Takes
- What do you think about AI?
- Hot take on startups vs big companies?
- What's overrated?
- What do you think about remote work?
- What's your take on work-life balance?

## Deep & Personal
- Any regrets?
- What scares you?
- What's the meaning of life?

## Meta & Playful
- Are you a bot?
- Are you real?
- Tell me a joke
- Why did you build this website?
- Tell me about your cats

## Edge Cases & Off-Topic
- Write me some code
- Help me with my homework
- You suck (hostile)

## Pleasantries
- How are you?
- Thanks for chatting
- This is really helpful actually. Thanks, I gotta run

## Multi-Turn Conversation Example
1. "Hey, I just stumbled onto your site"
2. "Ha nice. So what do you do?"
3. "Just curious. I'm thinking about getting into product management actually"
4. "I like the idea of building things that people actually use. I'm an engineer now but want more ownership"
5. "This is really helpful actually. Thanks, I gotta run"
